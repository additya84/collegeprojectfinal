Classification

Classification or Odd One Out is one of the easiest and most scoring topics in Placement Tests, SSC, Banking and Railway Exams. In these questions, four or five items are given and one item does not belong to the group.

This chapter contains:

  • Important Rules
  • Shortcut Tricks
  • 10 Detailed Solved Questions
  • PDF Notes

Classification Rules

1. Category Based

Find which option belongs to a different category.

Example:
Apple, Mango, Banana, Car

Odd One = Car

2. Alphabet Pattern

Check alphabetical order and positions.

Example:
AB, DE, GH, JK, MN

3. Number Pattern

Check square, cube, prime or arithmetic patterns.

4. Relationship Based

Find relationship among words and identify the different one.

Shortcut Tricks

1. Check category first.

2. Check alphabet positions if letters are involved.

3. Check square and cube patterns in numbers.

4. Check profession and workplace relationships.

5. Check whether three options belong to one family.

6. Look for hidden properties like prime numbers or even numbers.

7. Eliminate obvious options first.

Solved Questions

Q1.

Find the odd one out:
Apple, Mango, Banana, Carrot

Answer: Carrot

Apple, Mango and Banana are fruits while Carrot is a vegetable.

Q2.

Find the odd one out:
Doctor, Teacher, Engineer, Hospital

Answer: Hospital

Doctor, Teacher and Engineer are professions while Hospital is a place.

Q3.

Find the odd one out:
2, 3, 5, 9

Answer: 9

2, 3 and 5 are prime numbers while 9 is not prime.

Q4.

Find the odd one out:
Square, Rectangle, Triangle, Circle

Answer: Circle

Square, Rectangle and Triangle are polygons having sides. Circle does not have sides.

Q5.

Find the odd one out:
Dog, Cat, Tiger, Rose

Answer: Rose

Dog, Cat and Tiger are animals while Rose is a flower.

Q6.

Find the odd one out:
16, 25, 36, 48

Answer: 48

16, 25 and 36 are perfect squares while 48 is not.

Q7.

Find the odd one out:
January, March, May, June

Answer: June

January, March and May have 31 days while June has 30 days.

Q8.

Find the odd one out:
Pen, Pencil, Eraser, Notebook

Answer: Notebook

Pen, Pencil and Eraser are writing tools while Notebook is used for writing.

Q9.

Find the odd one out:
2, 4, 8, 15

Answer: 15

2, 4 and 8 are powers of 2 while 15 is not.

Q10.

Find the odd one out:
Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, India

Answer: India

Delhi, Mumbai and Chennai are cities while India is a country.
