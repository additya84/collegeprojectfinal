Resume Based Interview Questions

Most interviewers spend the first few minutes carefully reading your resume and asking questions based on it. Therefore, you should know every single detail written in your resume.

Many students add skills, projects and technologies in their resume but fail to explain them during interviews. This creates a negative impression on interviewers.

Rule:

Never write anything on your resume that you cannot explain.

  • Education Questions
  • Skill Based Questions
  • Project Questions
  • Internship Questions
  • Certification Questions

Education And Skills Questions

1. Tell Me About Your Educational Background.

Interviewers ask this question to understand your academic journey. Example Answer:

"I completed my schooling with a focus on science and computer subjects. Currently, I am pursuing Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Dronacharya PG College, Rait under Himachal Pradesh University. During my studies I developed a strong interest in software development and web technologies."

2. Why Did You Choose BCA?

A good answer should explain your interest in computers and technology. Example:

"I chose BCA because I have always been interested in computers, programming and software development. The course helped me build strong fundamentals in programming, databases and web development."

3. Explain The Skills Mentioned In Your Resume.

If your resume contains:

HTML
CSS
JavaScript
React
ASP.NET
SQL
You should be able to explain:

• What the technology is.
• Why it is used.
• Where you used it.
• Projects built using it.

Project Based Questions

4. Explain Your Final Year Project.

This is one of the most important questions for freshers. Example:

"My final year project is a Placement Preparation Portal. The main objective of this project is to help students prepare for placements by providing aptitude questions, reasoning topics, technical interview questions and study materials. The frontend was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The project helped me improve my frontend development and UI designing skills."

5. What Challenges Did You Face During Development?

Example:

"One of the biggest challenges was organizing large amounts of content and creating a responsive user interface. I solved this problem by dividing the project into modules and improving component structure."

6. If Given More Time, What Improvements Would You Add?

Possible improvements:

• User Login System
• Mock Tests
• Progress Tracking
• Resume Builder
• AI Interview Assistant

Resume Tips

✔ Keep resume limited to 1 page for freshers.

✔ Use professional formatting.

✔ Highlight projects and skills.

✔ Mention achievements and certifications.

✔ Keep contact details updated.

✔ Use action words such as Developed, Designed, Built and Implemented.

Common Resume Mistakes

❌ Adding fake skills.

❌ Spelling mistakes.

❌ Writing long paragraphs.

❌ Including unnecessary personal details.

❌ Using unprofessional email addresses.

❌ Mentioning technologies that cannot be explained.
