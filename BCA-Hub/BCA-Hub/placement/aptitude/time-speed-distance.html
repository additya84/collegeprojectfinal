Time, Speed & Distance

Time, Speed and Distance is one of the most important topics for Placement Aptitude, SSC, Banking, Railway and CAT exams. Questions are frequently asked from trains, boats and streams, races, relative speed, average speed and circular track concepts.

A strong understanding of this chapter helps in solving many arithmetic problems quickly and accurately.

Shortcut Tricks

1. Ratio Method

If distance remains constant, Speed and Time are inversely proportional.

Speed Ratio = a : b
Time Ratio = b : a

Example:
Speed ratio = 4 : 3
Time ratio = 3 : 4

Solved Questions

Q1. A car travels 240 km in 4 hours. Find its speed.

Speed = Distance / Time

Speed = 240 / 4
Speed = 60 km/h

Answer = 60 km/h

Q2. A train 180 meters long crosses a pole in 9 seconds. Find the speed of train.

Speed = Length / Time

Speed = 180 / 9
Speed = 20 m/s

Converting into km/h:
20 ├Ś 18/5 = 72 km/h

Answer = 72 km/h

Q3. A man walks at 5 km/h and reaches office 12 minutes late. If he walks at 6 km/h, he reaches on time. Find distance.

Time Difference = 12 min = 0.2 hour

Distance = (5 ├Ś 6 ├Ś 0.2)/(6 - 5)

Distance = 6 km

Answer = 6 km

Q4. A train crosses a platform of length 220 m in 20 seconds. Length of train is 180 m. Find speed of train.

Total Distance = 220 + 180
Total Distance = 400 m

Speed = 400 / 20
Speed = 20 m/s
20 ├Ś 18/5 = 72 km/h

Answer = 72 km/h

Q5. A boat travels downstream at 15 km/h and upstream at 9 km/h. Find speed of boat in still water.

Boat Speed = (15 + 9)/2
Boat Speed = 12 km/h

Answer = 12 km/h

Q6. Two trains of lengths 150 m and 250 m are running in opposite directions at speeds of 54 km/h and 72 km/h respectively. Find the time taken to cross each other.

Total Length = 150 + 250
Total Length = 400 m

Convert speeds into m/s:
54 km/h = 15 m/s
72 km/h = 20 m/s

Relative Speed = 15 + 20
Relative Speed = 35 m/s

Time = Total Length / Relative Speed
Time = 400 / 35
Time = 11.43 seconds

Answer = 11.43 seconds

Q7. A man covers a distance at 40 km/h and returns at 60 km/h. Find his average speed for the whole journey.

Average Speed Formula:
Average Speed = 2xy / (x+y)

Average Speed = (2 ├Ś 40 ├Ś 60) / (40 + 60)

Average Speed = 4800 / 100
Average Speed = 48 km/h

Answer = 48 km/h

Q8. Two runners are running around a circular track of length 400 m at speeds of 8 m/s and 12 m/s in the same direction. After how much time will they meet again?

Relative Speed = 12 - 8
Relative Speed = 4 m/s

Time = Length of Track / Relative Speed
Time = 400 / 4
Time = 100 seconds

Answer = 100 seconds

Q9. A bus covers 300 km in 5 hours including stoppages. If the running speed of the bus is 75 km/h, find the stoppage time per hour.

Actual Speed = 75 km/h
Average Speed = 300 / 5
Average Speed = 60 km/h

Stoppage Time per Hour = ((75 - 60) / 75) ├Ś 60

= (15 / 75) ├Ś 60
= 12 minutes

Answer = 12 minutes per hour

= 9 km/h

Stream Speed = (12 - 9) / 2
