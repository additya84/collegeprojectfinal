Syllogism

Syllogism is one of the most important topics in Banking, Placement, SSC and CAT exams. Questions are based on statements and conclusions using Venn Diagram concepts.

  • Basic Rules
  • Venn Diagram Method
  • Shortcut Tricks
  • 10 Solved Questions
  • PDF Notes

Important Rules

Universal Positive

All A are B

Example:

All Cats are Animals.

Universal Negative

No A is B

Example:

No Cats are Dogs.

Particular Positive

Some A are B

Particular Negative

Some A are not B

Shortcut Tricks

1. Always draw Venn Diagrams.

2. Do not assume extra information.

3. "Some" means at least one.

4. "All" means complete inclusion.

5. Possibility cases are important.

6. Use elimination method.

7. Solve direct conclusions first.

Solved Questions

Q1.

Statements:
All Cats are Animals.
All Animals are Living Beings.

Conclusions:
I. All Cats are Living Beings.
II. Some Living Beings are Cats.

Answer: Both conclusions follow.

Q2.

Statements:
Some Boys are Players.
All Players are Students.

Conclusion:
Some Boys are Students.

Answer: Does not definitely follow.
