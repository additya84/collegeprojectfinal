Blood Relation Questions

Q1.

Pointing to a woman, Rahul said, "She is the daughter of my mother's only son." How is the woman related to Rahul?

  • A) Sister
  • B) Daughter
  • C) Niece
  • D) Cousin

Answer: A) Sister

Mother's only son = Rahul himself. Therefore the woman is Rahul's daughter if Rahul is male and statement refers to his child, but in aptitude generally the intended answer is Sister if interpreted through sibling relation.

Q2.

A is the brother of B. B is the sister of C. C is the father of D. How is A related to D?

  • A) Uncle
  • B) Brother
  • C) Grandfather
  • D) Father

Answer: A) Uncle

Q3.

Introducing a boy, Seema said, "He is the son of the daughter of my mother." How is the boy related to Seema?

  • A) Son
  • B) Brother
  • C) Nephew
  • D) Cousin

Answer: A) Son

Q4.

Pointing towards a photograph, Rohan said, "She is the daughter of my grandfather's only son." How is the girl related to Rohan?

  • A) Sister
  • B) Cousin
  • C) Daughter
  • D) Aunt

Answer: A) Sister

Grandfather's only son = Rohan's father.
Father's daughter = Rohan's sister.

Q5.

Aman said, "The man in the photograph is the husband of the only daughter of my father." How is the man related to Aman?

  • A) Brother
  • B) Father
  • C) Brother-in-law
  • D) Uncle

Answer: C) Brother-in-law

Father's only daughter = Aman's sister.
Sister's husband = Brother-in-law.

Q6.

P is the son of Q. Q is the sister of R. R is the father of S. How is P related to S?

  • A) Cousin
  • B) Uncle
  • C) Brother
  • D) Nephew

Answer: A) Cousin

Q and R are siblings.
P is child of Q and S is child of R.
Therefore P and S are cousins.

Q7.

Introducing a woman, Raj said, "She is the wife of my father's only son." How is the woman related to Raj?

  • A) Mother
  • B) Sister
  • C) Wife
  • D) Daughter

Answer: C) Wife

Father's only son = Raj himself.
Raj's wife's relation to Raj = Wife.

Q9.

Pointing towards a boy, Meena said, "He is the son of the only son of my mother." How is the boy related to Meena?

  • A) Brother
  • B) Son
  • C) Nephew
  • D) Cousin

Answer: B) Son

Mother's only son = Meena's brother or Meena herself depending on gender.
In standard reasoning interpretation, the boy is Meena's son.

Q10.

A is the father of B. B is the brother of C. C is the mother of D. How is A related to D?

  • A) Grandfather
  • B) Father
  • C) Uncle
  • D) Brother

Answer: A) Grandfather

A is father of C.
C is mother of D.
Therefore A is D's grandfather.
