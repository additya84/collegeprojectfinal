Seating Arrangement

Seating Arrangement is one of the most important and high-weightage topics in Placement Tests, Banking, SSC and CAT exams. Questions are based on linear arrangement, circular arrangement, facing direction and position based reasoning.

Types of Seating Arrangement

Linear Arrangement

People sit in a straight line facing North or South.

Circular Arrangement

People sit around a table or circle.

Square Arrangement

People sit at corners or sides of a square.

Rectangular Arrangement

People sit around a rectangle facing inside or outside.

Shortcut Tricks

1. Draw positions immediately.

2. Fix the first person to reduce possibilities.

3. Use arrows for facing direction.

4. Facing center means Left = Clockwise.

5. Facing outside means Left = Anti-clockwise.

6. Solve fixed positions first.

7. Use elimination method for remaining seats.

Solved Questions

Q1.

A, B, C, D and E are sitting in a row. A is to the left of B and C is to the right of D. Who is sitting in the middle?

Answer: C

Q2.

Five friends sit around a circular table facing center. If A is immediately right of B, who sits immediately left of B?

Answer: Depends on complete arrangement.

Q3.

P is second to the left of Q. R is third to the right of Q. Who is between P and R?

Answer: Q

Q4.

Six people sit in a row facing North. A sits at one end and B sits second from the other end. How many people sit between them?

Answer: 3

Q5.

In a circular arrangement facing center, who is on your left if you turn clockwise?

Answer: Clockwise neighbour.

Q6.

Eight persons sit around a circle facing center. A sits opposite B. How many persons are between them?

Answer: 3

Q7.

Seven friends sit in a row. R is exactly in the middle. How many persons are on each side of R?

Answer: 3

Q8.

A sits to the immediate right of B. B sits opposite C. Where is C with respect to A?

Answer: Opposite Left.

Q9.

In a row of 10 students, Rahul is fifth from left and Amit is third from right. How many students sit between them?

Answer: 1

Q10.

Eight people sit around a circular table facing outside. Who is on your left side?

Answer: Anti-clockwise neighbour.

Image Based Question
