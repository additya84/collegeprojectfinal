Technical Interview Preparation

Technical interviews are conducted to evaluate a candidate's technical knowledge, problem solving ability and understanding of core concepts related to software development.

For BCA students, interviewers generally focus on programming fundamentals, databases, web development, object oriented programming and projects.

The purpose of technical interviews is not to test whether you know everything but to evaluate how you approach problems and explain concepts.
