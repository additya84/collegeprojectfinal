Professional Resume Template For Freshers

A resume is your first impression during placements and job interviews. A well structured resume increases your chance of getting shortlisted significantly.

Recruiters usually spend less than 10 seconds scanning a resume before deciding whether to continue reading it or move to the next candidate.

This page explains every important section that should be included in a professional fresher resume.

1. Personal Information

This section contains your basic information so recruiters can contact you easily.

Example

Name: ABC

Email: adI@gmail.com

Phone: +91 XXXXX XXXXX

Location: Himachal Pradesh, India

LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in

GitHub: github.com/additya

2. Career Objective

The objective section tells recruiters what type of role you are looking for and what value you can provide.

Motivated BCA student with strong interest in software development and web technologies seeking an opportunity to apply technical skills and problem solving abilities in a professional environment.

3. Education

Mention your latest qualification first.

Bachelor of Computer Applications

Dronacharya PG College, Rait

HP University

2023 - 2026

CGPA: 7.69

12th Standard

HP Board

20XX

4. Technical Skills

Only include skills that you can confidently explain during interviews.

HTML CSS JavaScript React C# ASP.NET SQL Git

5. Projects

Placement Preparation Portal

Developed a portal containing aptitude questions, reasoning material and interview preparation resources.

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

6. Internship Experience

If you do not have an internship, mention workshops, training programs or academic projects.

Resume Tips

  • Keep resume within one page.
  • Use professional email address.
  • Highlight projects and skills.
  • Use proper formatting.
  • Proofread before sending.

Common Mistakes

  • Adding fake skills.
  • Grammar mistakes.
  • Long paragraphs.
  • Irrelevant information.
