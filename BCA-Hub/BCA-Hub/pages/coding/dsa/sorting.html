Sorting Algorithms

Learn various sorting algorithms including Bubble Sort, Selection Sort, Merge Sort and Quick Sort.

📘 What is Sorting?

Sorting is the process of arranging data in ascending or descending order.

Example: 50 20 10 40 30 → 10 20 30 40 50
